Welcome to CatMap!

The aim of this projects is to visualize the territory and movements of my cats.
To achieve this I equipped them with GPS loggers and exported the resulting data as a GeoJSON object.
Get started by picking a cat. Afterwards you can use the time slider to see the cats position at the selected time.
Feel free to drag, rotate and zoom around.

NOTE: Currently there is only one dataset available for each cat. More will follow in future updates.

This project is part of the course »Information Visualization WS 2016 at FH Potsdam«. Supervised by Prof. Dr. Marian Dörk. Created by Arthur Schiller.
